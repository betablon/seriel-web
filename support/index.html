Manage Your Subscription

Seriel subscriptions are managed through Apple. To view, modify, or cancel your subscription:

  • On your iPhone, open Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions
  • Find Seriel in the list and tap to manage

You can also manage your subscriptions directly through Apple: Manage Subscriptions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trakt.tv and why do I need an account?

Trakt.tv is a free service that keeps track of which TV shows and episodes you’ve watched. Seriel uses your Trakt account to store and sync your watch history, ratings, and watchlist. You can create a free account at trakt.tv. Without a Trakt account, Seriel cannot function.

How do I mark an episode as watched?

Open a show, scroll to the episode list, and tap the circle icon on the right side of the episode row. If you’re catching up, Seriel will ask if you’d like to mark all previous episodes as watched too — so you don’t have to tap each one individually.

I watched episodes on Trakt.tv directly — why don’t they show up?

Seriel syncs with Trakt every time you open the app. If changes still don’t appear, try opening the specific show — this triggers a detailed refresh. You can also force a full re-import from Settings.

What is the watchlist?

The watchlist is a list of shows you plan to watch in the future. You can add any show to your watchlist by tapping the bookmark icon on the show’s detail page. Your watchlist syncs with Trakt, so shows you add on trakt.tv or other apps will appear in Seriel too.

How do ratings work?

You can rate individual episodes and entire shows on a 1–5 star scale. Seriel converts this to Trakt’s 1–10 scale automatically. Your ratings sync with Trakt and will be visible on trakt.tv. After completing a season, Seriel will prompt you to rate the show.

What does “Up Next” mean?

The Up Next section on the home screen shows the next unwatched episode for each of your tracked shows. It updates automatically as you mark episodes as watched, so you always know exactly where you left off.

Can I use Seriel without an internet connection?

You can browse shows and episodes you’ve previously loaded, but marking episodes as watched or syncing changes requires an internet connection. Any actions taken offline will be synced once you’re back online.

How do I remove a show from my tracked shows?

Open the show’s detail page, tap the bookmark icon to remove it from your watchlist, or use the options menu (three dots) for more actions.

Will I get notifications for new episodes?

Yes. Seriel can notify you when new episodes become available, when a new season is announced, or when a show’s status changes (e.g., cancelled or returning). You can enable or disable each notification type individually in Settings.

How do I get my data if I switch to a new phone?

Since all your watch history and ratings are stored on Trakt.tv, simply sign in with the same Trakt account on your new device. Seriel will import everything automatically during setup.
